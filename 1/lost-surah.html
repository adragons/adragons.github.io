Piyaz Sal-Ad
Hear! O believers, who were created by a drop of sperm
And who wear clothes
And who establish prayer
And who believe in Allah
Allah has sent you a prophet, Muhammad (peace be upon him), most redolent,
to remind the believers, and the polytheists, and the hypocrites
that Allah is most wise
And Allah hath chosen [him] wisely
Indeed Allah is over all things competent
And surely we vouched for him
And surely we blessed him with the wind of roses.
It is he who smelteth it, he who dealteth it!
Yet those disbelievers of this are cursed.
And they say "Is it not he who denieth it, he who supplyeth it?"
But Muhammad (PBUH) is meticulous and said:
"It is he who observereth it, he who serveth it!"
And still the disbelievers refuse, for they are habitual liars
"He soever rebuts it, cuts it"
Unquestionably, the disbelievers are evil
And their abode is hell.
